Browse and analyze datasets

Datasets can be visualized and analyzed with our tools that are specific for parameter search, but also with your custom code, all on the web! You can also access other public datasets, such that you can ask novel questions with them. Use them to guide your own research, or simply start new collaborations. You can join existing teams, or start your own.

Publish your own analyses

We allow making your own copies by filtering others' datasets. You can also duplicate their analyses and improve them by making them your own. This facilitates crowdsourcing scientific problems - especially when they are so large and complex as in parameter search!

Easily share parameter search datasets

Share, either privately or publicly, your tabular data that has parameters and measurements from any model or experiment. Enter minimal metadata to let others know what your columns mean. We will make your data accessible via our web interface for humans and via a standard RESTful web API for robotic integration. Ideal for publication requirements and collaboration.
